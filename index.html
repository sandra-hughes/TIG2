Flux Grid Arena

A 45-second rhythm reaction game. Hit glowing targets fast to build your combo and score. Tap a rift or an empty cell and you lose points instantly.

Click start to generate a new run.

How To Play

  • Pulse is the standard score tile. Faster reactions grant more points.
  • Echo expires faster but pays out more score per hit.
  • Rift is a trap. Hitting it costs points and resets your combo.
  • Missing a score tile also breaks rhythm and lowers your accuracy.
